Goals

The site is deemed to encourage more individuals and even families to sign-up for membership also encourage them to take part in voluntary services.
To familiarize the localites with services that Townsville Community Music Centre offers. Thus even a novice artist or a newcomer can avail the
services and facilities of the centre.

Success Evaluation

Within 90 days after website goes live, Townsville Community Music Centre aims to:

  • Recruit new members at least increase the members strength by 50 percent.
  • Increase engagement of members. ie. volunteering for administration. After the sign-up, at least 75 percent of new joinees
    should start volunteering work and give a helping hand to administration.
  • Increase the ticket sales, especially among general public by 40 percent. More music concerts will be organised as a result of promotional
    activities by new volunteers & artists, more tickets will get sold which inturn contributes to the revenue.
  • Encourage performers to cooperate with promotion, photo shoots, media releases, etc on the behalf of Music Centre.
    Use this revamped website as a platform for future promotion of all events thereby increase ticket sales by 30 percent.

Along with that Music Centre can reach out from any region through website. It can update and upload information
as per the requirement of the members. Also with the help of contact form a customer can query related to various
matters or can join the events.

Target Audience

The client base is wide and with diverse music tastes, age group consists of children to senior audiences.The new music centre website
will have appealing designs and the spaces are utilized equally for whole events to cater multifaceted clients.

Content

  • Home page- The landing page and the content may change depending on the season and featured events
  • Musos - Details of local home grown talent and musicians.
  • Membership - Sign in page for enrolling new members.
  • About Us - Tells about the organisation and humble beginnings
  • Contact Us - Contains address, Location map, contact numbers, email address
